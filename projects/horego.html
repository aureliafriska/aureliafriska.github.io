Project Overview

The Challenge

B2B F&B companies grapple with inefficiencies stemming from disjointed supply chain systems—spanning inventory management, procurement, transportation, and order tracking. Horego addresses this challenge by integrating these systems into a unified platform, streamlining processes, minimizing redundancies, and enabling businesses to optimize operations for enhanced efficiency and cost-effectiveness. By consolidating all supply chain activities, Horego empowers F&B SMEs to overcome complexity and concentrate on propelling growth and success.

The Goals

Establish Horego as the go-to platform for F&B businesses looking to optimize their supply chain management and achieve greater efficiency and profitability by simplifying bulk orders, delivery tracking, inventory management, and process streamlining through a user-friendly interface.

Understanding Users

Competitive Analysis

Conducting a competitive audit is crucial to analyze competitors thoroughly, identifying potential opportunities and threats to our platform's success, including aspects such as target audience, business model, traffic analysis, ordering processes, unique features, pricing strategies, and social media presence. Extracting valuable insights helps us discern effective industry practices, utilize SWOT analysis, and tailor our platform to meet the distinct needs of our target audience. Noteworthy competitors include Esbgoods, Zeemart, Supplynote, Foodmarkethub, and Hyperpure.

Target Groups

Our main focus for Horego comprises business owners in Indonesia aged 25-44. The central objective of our value proposition canvas is to cater to those seeking a solution to streamline their supply chain operations and enhance overall business efficiency.
